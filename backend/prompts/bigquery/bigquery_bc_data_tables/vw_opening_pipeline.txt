**Table Structure**
--------------------
**Table Name: vw_opening_pipeline**
   **Description:** Represents the opening pipeline data, including opportunity details, account information, and financial metrics. It is used to analyze the initial stages of the sales pipeline.
 
            Amount_converted_Currency (STRING): The currency in which the converted amount is denominated.
            Amount_converted (FLOAT): The converted amount in a specific currency for each record.
            Fiscal_Period (STRING): The fiscal period in which the data entry belongs to, represented in a quarter-year format.
            Opportunity_Name (STRING): The name of the opportunity or project associated with each record in the pipeline.
            Opportunity_Owner (STRING): The individual responsible for managing the respective sales opportunities.
            Stage (STRING): The current stage of the pipeline process for each record.
            Type (STRING): The type of pipeline activity, such as new deals, renewals, TCV renewals, or reconciliations for finance.
            Close_Date (DATE): The date when the pipeline opportunities were closed.
            Department (STRING): The department within the organization to which a project or team belongs.
            Sub_Department (STRING): The sub-department within the organization's technology and data-related divisions.
            Sales_Type (STRING): The type of sales method used for each pipeline entry.
            Customer_Type (STRING): The type of customer status associated with each record in the pipeline.
            Account_Owner (STRING): The account owner responsible for managing each record in the pipeline.
            Age (INTEGER): The age of individuals in the pipeline.
            Stage_Duration (INTEGER): The duration in days of each stage in the pipeline process.
            Created_Date (DATE): The date when the pipeline entry was created.
            Fiscal_Year (INTEGER): The fiscal year in which the pipeline data was recorded.
            Client_Partner (STRING): The primary client partner assigned to each record in the opening pipeline.
            Account_Category_New (STRING): The new category classification of accounts related to farming and hunting activities.
            BO_I_Forecast (STRING): The forecast status of a business opportunity, indicated by colors such as "Green" and "Red".
            Total_Studio (INTEGER): The total number of studio spaces available for booking in the pipeline.
            SC_platform (FLOAT): The percentage completion of a specific platform within the opening pipeline process.
            SAP_Studio (FLOAT): The SAP Studio's utilization percentage in the opening pipeline process.
            Emerging_Platforms (FLOAT): The percentage of progress made in emerging platforms for each record.
            Kinaxis_Studio (FLOAT): The numeric value representing the percentage completion in the Kinaxis Studio for each record in the pipeline.
            Integration (FLOAT): The percentage of integration completion for each pipeline stage.
            SC_Build_Enablement1 (FLOAT): The percentage completion of a specific task related to SC Build Enablement.
            Solution_Consulting1 (FLOAT): The numerical value representing the level of solution consulting engagement for each record.
            Commercial_BU (STRING): The business unit or industry segment associated with each record in the pipeline.
            Account (STRING): The name of the company associated with each record in the opening pipeline.
            Sales_Forecast_Category (STRING): The level of certainty for sales forecasts, ranging from "Possible" to "Closed".
            Opportunity_Status (STRING): The current status of opportunities in the pipeline, including values such as "Open", "Closed", "Abandoned", and "Lost".
