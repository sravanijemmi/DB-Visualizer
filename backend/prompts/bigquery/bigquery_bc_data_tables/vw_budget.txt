**Table Structure**
--------------------
**Table Name: vw_budget**
   **Description:** Stores budget allocation data across departments and account types, along with anonymized identifiers and monthly breakdowns.
 
            Department (STRING): The department within the organization to which the budget allocation is assigned.
            Account_Name (STRING): The name of the account associated with budget allocations.
            Commercial_BU_Name (STRING): The commercial business unit name associated with the entity's operations.
            Client_partner (STRING): The primary partner associated with the budget allocation for various clients.
            Account_Category_Type (STRING): The type of account category related to farming or hunting activities.
            Type (STRING): The type of budget, indicating whether it is a new deal or a renewal.
            Fiscal Year (STRING): The fiscal year to which the budget data corresponds.
            April (FLOAT): The column `April` in the `vw_budget` table represents the budget amounts allocated for the month of April.
            May (FLOAT): The column `May` represents the budget amounts for the entity in the month of May.
            June (FLOAT): The column `June` represents the budget amount allocated for the month of June.
            July (FLOAT): The column `July` represents the budget amount allocated for the month of July.
            August (FLOAT): The column `August` represents the budget amount allocated for the month of August in the financial dataset.
            September (FLOAT): The estimated budget amount allocated for the month of September.
            October (FLOAT): The financial budget amount allocated for the month of October.
            November (FLOAT): The estimated budget amount for the month of November.
            December (FLOAT): The financial budget allocated for the month of December.
            January (FLOAT): The column `January` represents the budget amount allocated for the month of January.
            February (FLOAT): The estimated budget amount allocated for the month of February.
            March (FLOAT): The estimated budget allocation for the month of March.

**Example-1**
--------------
**User Input**: Retrieve accounts with a Type of 'Renewal' and Account Category Type of 'Farming'.
**AI Response**:
                SELECT DISTINCT 
                    Account_Name, Client_Partner 
                FROM 
                    vw_budget
                WHERE 
                    Type = 'Renewal' 
                        AND Account_Category_Type = 'Farming'
                ORDER BY 
                    Account_Name;
