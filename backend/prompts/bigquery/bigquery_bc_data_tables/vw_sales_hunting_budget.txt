**Table Structure**
--------------------
**Table Name: vw_sales_hunting_budget**
   **Description:** Captures hunting budget allocations across fiscal years, regions, and account types, including opportunity owners. It provides monthly budget distribution from April to March, enabling analysis of sales hunting performance and tracking of budgeted amounts for new deals and renewals.
 
            Fiscal Year (STRING): The fiscal year associated with the hunting budget record.
            Region (STRING): The geographic region where the hunting budget sales are allocated.
            Type (STRING): The type of sales transaction, categorized as either "Renewal" or "New Deal".
            Accounts (STRING): The names of various client accounts associated with hunting budget sales.
            Opportunity Owner (STRING): The individual responsible for managing sales opportunities within the hunting budget.
            April (FLOAT): The column `April` represents the budget amounts allocated for sales hunting activities in the month of April.
            May (FLOAT): The budget allocated for the month of May in the sales hunting activities, stored as floating-point numbers.
            June (FLOAT): The column `June` represents the budget amounts allocated for hunting sales in June, stored as floating-point numbers.
            July (FLOAT): The July column represents the budget allocated for hunting sales activities in the month of July.
            August (FLOAT): The budget allocated for hunting sales in August in various amounts.
            September (FLOAT): The budget allocated for September sales hunting activities in monetary values.
            October (FLOAT): The budget allocated for October sales activities in floating-point format.
            November (FLOAT): The budget allocated for November sales hunting activities in floating-point format.
            December (FLOAT): The budget allocated for December sales initiatives in floating point format.
            January (FLOAT): The budget allocated for sales hunting activities in the month of January.
            February (FLOAT): The budget allocated for sales hunting activities in February, represented in floating-point numbers.
            March (FLOAT): The budget allocated for the month of March in the sales hunting activities.
